## comparison
```
/kəm'pærɪsn/
n. 比较，对照

The findings were based on the comparison of the two styles.

研究结果建立在对两种风格的对比之上。

in comparison with 与……比较
```

## comparative
```
/kəm'pærətɪv/
adj. 比较的

The team conducted a comparative study of the two systems.
该团队开展了这两项制度的比较研究。
```
