## indication
```
/ˌɪndɪ'keɪʃn/
n. 显示, 表明, 标示, 象征, 暗示

Being unable to walk in a straight line is a clear indication of getting drunk.

无法走直线是醉酒的明显迹象。
```

## reveal
```
/rɪ'viːl/
v. 显示；表明；透露；揭露；展示；使显露
The report reveals that the company made a loss of £20 million last year.

报告显示，公司去年亏损了2000万英镑。
```
